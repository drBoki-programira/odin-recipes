Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Quick, easy and delicious mac'n'cheese.

Ingredients

  • 400g macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Cook macaroni in it for about 8 mins.
  2. At the same time melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt and pepper and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continuously. Cook for 5 mins, making sure milk doesn't burn.
  5. Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted.
  6. Drain macaroni and mix into cheese sauce.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
