Spicy Baked Shrimp

Description

Made with a perfect blend of spices. Finger licking delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 500g shrimp, peeled and deveined

Steps

  1. Whisk olive oil, Cajun seasoning, lemon juice, parsley, honey, soy sauce and cayenne pepper in a large bowl. Add shrimp and toss evenly to coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator for an hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 230 oC
  3. Transfer shrimp into the baking dish and pour marinade over top.
  4. Bake in oven until shrimp are bright pink, about 10 mins.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
